# Fashion, Influencer, And Meme Closure Results

Generated by `python3 scripts/run_fashion_meme_analysis.py`.

## Main Finding

A fashion or meme field is harmlessly continuous only below a critical
social-feedback threshold. Above that threshold, preference closure can
be multi-valued: a temporary influencer or meme shock can move the
economy from one stable taste state to another, and the new state can
persist after the shock disappears.
