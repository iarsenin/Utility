## Main Findings

3. Section 8 should explicitly say that \(\zeta\) from the general model is represented there by the scalar exposure field \(z\) or by a path of \(z\).
4. The manuscript should avoid pop phrasing when a precise phrase is easy: "targeted exposure field" is better than "influencers matter most."
5. The paper should repeat, near the social-feedback embedding, that these are conditional model results and not claims about every platform or fashion cycle.
